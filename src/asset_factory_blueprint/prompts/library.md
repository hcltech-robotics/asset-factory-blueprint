# Library

asset_library_search: search the curated, local and remote library indexes plus the configured USD Search endpoint for materials, textures, USD assets, exemplars and knowledge; use the results to ground every prim, material and property.

asset_library_index: scan the operator-declared local backings (material folders, texture folders, USD asset folders, the Omniverse estate) into searchable indexes.

asset_library_fetch: plan or perform downloads of query-responsive items or whole packs from indexed remote sources into the library cache.
