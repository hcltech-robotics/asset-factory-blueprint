# Code of conduct

This project welcomes technically rigorous disagreement and expects it to remain professional.

## Expected conduct

- Discuss evidence, interfaces and outcomes rather than attacking people.
- State assumptions, conflicts of interest and uncertainty that affect a review.
- Respect privacy, licences and restrictions attached to source assets.
- Give clear, actionable feedback and allow contributors a reasonable opportunity to respond.
- Stop contact when another participant asks you to stop.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory conduct, deliberate disclosure of private information and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Include links or copies of the relevant material and any immediate safety concern. Do not open a public issue about an individual.

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation or ban an account. A maintainer named in a report must not adjudicate it. Reports are shared only with the people needed to investigate and act.
